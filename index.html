About IETE Students' Forum of SIESGST

The IETE Students' Forum of SIES GST, Nerul, was inaugurated in March 2008. This Student Chapter plays an active role in organizing extra-curricular events for the college. ISF believes in originality and has strived to always incorporate new and innovative ideas in all projects and activities. Our motto is to encourage teamwork and the spark to wander into newer and unexplored territories in our student members.

The major events held under the banner of ISF are Techxter- A national level technical paper presentation competition; Ideation- An idea presentation competition; Bootcamps on various topics; Seminars- on upcoming technologies. ISF organized an Idea presentation competition in collaboration with various NGOs. In this competition, students were given various problem statements pertaining to various social issues faced by them.

ISF also conducted a Bootcamp to ISME, One Indiabulls, Parel. Topics such as Data Science, Design Thinking etc. were discussed in this Bootcamp by experts from various fields.

Apart from competitions, ISF organizes workshops on trending technologies to expose students to concepts and tools apart from the prescribed syllabus. Workshops such as GitHub, ML, Python etc. were conducted.

Our Aims and objectives :
‣ To plan, organize Technical Programs, Special Lectures, Workshops, Seminars, Symposia, exhibitions for the benefit of students.
‣ To provide a common platform for students to exchange ideas in technical topics of interest, e.g., curriculum, employment, higher educational opportunities, emerging trends, etc.
‣ To facilitate technical visits, project works, employment, contact with industries and academic institutions.
‣ Encourage team spirit and self-reliance among student members.

Events

Ideation

Ideation is an ideathon organized by the IETE Students' Forum of SIES Graduate School of Technology. It serves as a platform for enthusiasts to generate creative and technically productive ideas to address real-life problems faced by society. The event encourages aspiring minds and innovators to present solutions ranging from technology-driven concepts to humanitarian initiatives, all aimed at making the world a better place. Its prime motto is to give back to society by solving commonly faced issues through technical ideas, which can be developed into practical projects. Organized in collaboration with NGOs such as the Child Help Foundation, the event focuses on problem statements in domains like environmental issues, sanitation, online safety, mental health, crime, gender discrimination, and natural disaster preparedness. The format typically includes an inauguration ceremony, brainstorming and mentoring sessions, idea presentations, and a valedictory ceremony.

Techxter

Techxter is a National Level Technical Paper Presentation hosted by the IETE Students' Forum of SIES Graduate School of Technology. Open to students from engineering, polytechnic, and other technical courses, the event provides a platform to present research and innovative ideas across diverse domains. Each edition receives paper submissions from institutions across India, covering various engineering and technology disciplines such as information technology, computer engineering, mechanical engineering, and electronics and telecommunication. Selected papers are presented in a competitive one-day event, featuring participants from both local and outstation colleges. The atmosphere is highly engaging, with discussions on a wide range of technical topics. The judging panel typically includes industry experts and senior faculty from reputed institutions, ensuring fair evaluation and representation of all engineering specialties. The event aims to encourage research culture, knowledge sharing, and innovation among students.

Projexions

PROJEXIONS is an annual technical showcase organized by the Department of Information Technology in collaboration with the IETE Students' Forum of SIES Graduate School of Technology. It serves as a platform for final-year IT students to present their work through a poster exhibition and for third-year students to demonstrate their skills via project presentations. The event covers diverse domains, including IoT, Android App Development, Web Development, and Artificial Intelligence, highlighting both technical innovation and social impact. Each domain undergoes evaluation by a panel of IT department faculty members. Participants are assessed on creativity, functionality, design, and the real-world relevance of their work. Winners are selected from each panel, ensuring recognition across all domains. The event typically features over 40 teams, divided into multiple panels for smooth execution. The day begins with a motivational address by the Head of the IT Department, inspiring students to use their skills for the betterment of society. True to this vision, the projects and posters often carry the underlying theme of "Giving back to Society", reflecting a blend of technical advancement and community service. PROJEXIONS not only celebrates student innovation but also fosters teamwork, presentation skills, and industry readiness.

Membership of IETE

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) is a leading professional society in India for electronics, telecommunications, IT, and related technical fields.

Established to promote advancements in these domains, IETE serves as a platform for students, academicians, and professionals to exchange ideas, explore emerging technologies, and enhance their technical expertise.

IETE membership provides students with an excellent opportunity to be part of this prestigious network, offering numerous benefits that foster professional growth, innovation, and learning beyond the classroom.

As a member, you gain access to workshops, seminars, technical competitions, and exclusive publications, while connecting with a community that thrives on technology and creativity.





Benefits of IETE Membership:


  • Discounts on workshops, seminars, and events conducted by IETE.

  • Priority consideration for publishing papers in IETE journals.

  • Complimentary subscription to IETE publications.

  • Eligibility to join the IETE Council and contribute to decision-making.

  • Opportunities to volunteer and network during major IETE events.
